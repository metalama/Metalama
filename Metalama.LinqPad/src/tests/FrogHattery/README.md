# 🐸 FrogHattery 🎩

> *"A frog without a hat is just a toad with ambition."*
> — Ancient Pond Proverb

## What Is This?

Welcome to **Le Chapeau de la Grenouille**, the world's premier (and only) frog millinery enterprise. We believe every amphibian deserves to look fabulous, regardless of species, toxicity level, or number of sticky toe pads.

## The Problem We're Solving

For too long, frogs have suffered in silence. While humans parade around in their fancy hats, our green friends have been left exposed to the elements, their heads cold and unfashionable.

Studies show that 9 out of 10 frogs feel *underdressed* at pond parties. The remaining 1 frog is a bullfrog who doesn't care about anything except defending his territory and eating crickets.

## Features

### 🐸 Frog Management
- **TreeFrog**: Small, agile, climbs trees, probably judges your lifestyle choices
- **BullFrog**: Large, loud, will fight you for a lilypad
- **PoisonDartFrog**: Beautiful but deadly, like that ex you still think about

### 🎩 Hat Collection
- **TopHat**: For the frog who wants to look like a tiny Victorian gentleman
- **Beret**: For the artistically inclined frog who ribbits in French
- **SunHat**: Because even cold-blooded creatures need UV protection
- **WinterCap**: With ear flaps! (Do frogs have ears? Don't think about it.)

### 🌦️ Seasonal Fashion Advice
Our `SeasonalFrogFashion` advisor ensures your frog never commits a fashion faux pas:
- **Spring**: Berets for the rain
- **Summer**: Wide brims for the sun
- **Autumn**: Top hats for falling leaf elegance
- **Winter**: Cozy caps before hibernation

## Quick Start

```csharp
var gerald = new BullFrog { Name = "Gerald", Age = 3 };
var topHat = new TopHat { Size = 8.0 };

gerald.PutOnHat(topHat);
// Output: "Gerald is now wearing a stylish Top Hat!"

gerald.Ribbit();
```
